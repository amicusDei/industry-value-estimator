<objective>
Build the walk-forward backtesting pipeline: assemble hard actuals (EDGAR direct-disclosure companies) and soft actuals (market anchor analyst consensus), run 3-fold walk-forward evaluation (train pre-2022, evaluate 2022-2024), compute MAPE/R2 separately for hard vs soft actuals, produce backtesting_results.parquet.

Purpose: MODL-06 — real out-of-sample validation with explicit hard/soft actual labels. This is a report-only system (not a gate) given the small-N limitation of 3 folds. MAPE thresholds are labels: <15% acceptable, 15-30% use with caution, >30% directional only.
Output: backtesting package (actuals_assembly.py + walk_forward.py), backtesting_results.parquet, pipeline wiring as Step 10.
</objective>
